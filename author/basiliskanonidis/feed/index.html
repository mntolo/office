Introducing, Project Management Addons!

Kanban boards


See work moving through multiple stages in Kanban board. Empower teams to self-manage with a better understanding of workflows as they get notified of tasks moving from one stage to another.

Timeline / Gantt charts


Use Timeline View to visualize and plan tasks to stay on your schedule. Set dependencies between tasks and easily adjust your plans as work changes and deadlines shift.

Google Doc Integration


Use your existing Google Docs folders to create a big library of documentation or file management inside Woffice.

Advanced Tasks


A more advanced view for your Woffice Interal view, allowing to see in details tasks. A new modern UI interface for this view also.
